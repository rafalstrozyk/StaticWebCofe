PowerCafe began with YOU in mind.

We know that people are hungry, not just physically either. All of us long to be part of something greater than ourselves and to experience soul-nourishment found in community.

The problem is that our lives are often over-scheduled with responsibility, creating little time for connection with others. This can leave you feeling stressed out and isolated. We get it. This is why we created a place with a comfortable atmosphere where you can disconnect from distractions and reconnect over a tasty meal. Not only that, but we’ve created a special “something” for each table to help you get the conversations started. We believe that your story is important, that nothing is wasted, and that everyone deserves a second chance. This is why you’ll find repurposed decor sprinkled into the atmosphere of PowerCafe. For example, the bricks used inside our building were part of The Great Chicago Fire. And, that old Army cot hanging in our event space? That has a story, too. So, we invite you to bring a friend (or several), leave the cooking to us, create a new memory, and experience the comfort of community in your locals’ living room.
